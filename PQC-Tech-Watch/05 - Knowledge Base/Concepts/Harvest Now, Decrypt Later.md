## Description
**"Harvest now, decrypt later"** (**HNDL**) is a surveillance strategy that relies on the acquisition and long-term storage of currently unreadable encrypted data awaiting possible breakthroughs in decryption technology that would render it readable in the future : a hypothetical date, the **Q-Day**.
