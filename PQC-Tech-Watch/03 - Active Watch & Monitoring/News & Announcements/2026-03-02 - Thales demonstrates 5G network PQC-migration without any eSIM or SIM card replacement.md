## Summary

**Thales** demonstrated the ability to remotely update post-quantum cryptographic algorithms on already-deployed 5G SIM and eSIM cards, with no service interruption and no physical replacement needed. This addresses one of the biggest operational challenges of the **PQC transition** for telecom operators managing billions of active SIM cards.

## Key Points

- Thales leverages **cryptographic agility** — the ability to swap security algorithms without hardware changes.
- Current 5G networks rely on **RSA** and **ECDH**, both vulnerable to **Shor's Algorithm**. 
- **NIST** finalized **CRYSTALS-Kyber** and **CRYSTALS-Dilithium** in 2024 as PQC standards — this demo shows they can be deployed on existing hardware.
- Reduces the gap between standard publication and real-world deployment.
