## Summary

The Trump administration released an official National Cybersecurity Strategy on March 7, 2026, explicitly promoting **post-quantum cryptography** adoption to protect U.S. digital infrastructure against **future quantum computing threats**. The strategy positions PQC alongside AI and blockchain security as key priorities for national security. This represents a major governmental endorsement and strategic commitment to PQC migration at the federal level.

## Key Points

- **Official Government Endorsement**: The Trump administration's National Cybersecurity Strategy officially promotes the adoption of post-quantum cryptography to protect against future quantum computing threats.
- **PQC as Strategic Priority**: Post-quantum cryptography is positioned alongside AI and blockchain security as core elements of U.S. digital infrastructure protection.
- **Infrastructure Protection**: The strategy explicitly recognizes the need to protect digital infrastructure against quantum computing threats that could emerge in the future
- **Cryptocurrency Security**: The strategy also addresses blockchain and cryptocurrency security, recognizing the need for quantum-resistant algorithms in financial systems.
- **Geopolitical Implication**: Signals American commitment to leading PQC adoption, potentially influencing international standards and corporate practices.
